About Us

Started as a side project in a small kitchen, Dough Nation ATX has grown into a citywide mission. We’re not a restaurant—we’re a movement fueled by heart, serving scratch-made pierogis to anyone in Austin who needs a warm meal. No questions asked, no barriers—just real food and human connection.

Our Impact

  • Over 1,000 meals served
  • 25+ locations across Austin
  • 100% community-driven

How It Works

We handcraft every pierogi with care, prioritizing bulk production to keep costs low. Our team distributes meals across Austin during off-hours, ensuring accessibility and dignity. It’s simple: no forms, no conditions—just warm food for all.
